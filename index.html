Research

My research focuses on the spatial and temporal dynamics of both human and anthropogenic disturbances in forested ecosystems using tree-rings. Teaching

I have experience teaching the following undergraduate or graduate courses:
Physical Geography, Geographic Information Systems 1 and 2, Applied GIS, Remote Sensing, and Advanced Data Analytics.

In the geospatial and data science courses I train students to use ESRI ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Google Earth Engine, and RStudio using a variety of languages including R, JavaScript, Phython, and SQL. Resources for some geospatial and data science courses can be found on my GitHub page.
